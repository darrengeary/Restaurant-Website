ABOUT US

A Family Run Restaurant.

McGlynn's is a licensed restaurant offering a variety of services including Casual dining, Takeaway, Outdoor Catering and Upstairs Party Venue with Catering.

We have been happily serving the local community for many years. Our dining restaurant is the perfect location for all day breakfast, lunch or family dinner.
