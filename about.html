About Us

ABOUT US

A Family Run Restaurant.

We have been happily serving the local community for many years. Below are the services we offer.

  • Dining Restaurant.
  • Upstairs Party Venue.
  • Licenced to sell and serve alcohol.
  • Takeaway Restaurant.
  • Outside Event Catering.
  • All Day Breakfast from 12pm

Party Venue

80 / 100 Guests

Our Restaurant can cater for parties up to around 80/100 people. We can cater for any event including christenings, communion, confirmations, birthdays, wedding anniversarys and funerals. You may request prepared meals for guests and refreshments can be served at the event. Please contact us if you would like to hear more.

Contact Us

Outdoor Catering

We offer a wide range of outdoor catering for your guests. We can provide dinners, finger food / sandwiches, cold meat salad buffets with chicken curry / rice or beef stroganoff. We can also cater to special dietary requests if needed. For more information please contact us to enquire about all options available.
